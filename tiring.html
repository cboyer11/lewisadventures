Ways To Tire Out Your Pup

Welcome to the Tiring page! Here, you'll find all the fun places me and my human love to go.

Hiking in Arizona

  • Gateway Loop TrailLocated at the McDowell Sonoran Preserve in Scottsdale, you are able to take your dog on may of their hikes on leash.
  • Lost Dog Wash TrailThis trail is fairly moderate and easy for you and a Pup, and gorgeous views.
  • Tom's Thumb TrailheadThere are multiple trails you are able to go on, some range from easy to very hard. Certain times of day you will have shade.

Coronado Dog Beach, San Deigo

    This is a dog-friendly beach where your pup can run off-leash and play in the sand. It is a great place to socialize with other dogs and enjoy the ocean breeze. We wear a life vest because the waves can get big, and we have little legs!

Enjoy some fun activities with your dog, remeber its their first time living too, and they only get to see what we show them!
