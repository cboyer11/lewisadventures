    Tiring

    Sniffspot is an amazing platform that allows me to explore new and exciting outdoor locations with my human. It's like having access to a private park where I can run and play off-leash.

    Snuffle Mats are fantastic enrichment toys that provide both mental and physical stimulation. They encourage natural foraging behavior and keep pups entertained.

    Orange treat toys are great for keeping me engaged and active. They dispense treats while encouraging problem solving.

    Tiring Activities — Quick Reference

    Activity Location Typical Duration Notes
    Sniffwalking Local parks / trails 20–45 minutes Let Lewis lead — slows pace and mentally tires pups.
    Off-leash play Sniffspot private yards 30–60 minutes Great for high-energy dogs; supervise around other dogs.
    Enrichment feeding (snuffle mat) Home 10–20 minutes Good for mental stimulation and slowing eating.
    Tug + treat puzzle Home / yard 15–30 minutes Combine physical and mental work for extra tiredness.
